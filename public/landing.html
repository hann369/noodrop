What to avoid

Optimization starts
with subtraction.

The biggest gains often come from removing threats, not adding supplements.

⬡
Microplastics

Nanoplastics have been detected in human brain tissue. Research on neuroinflammation and cognitive impact is still emerging — but accumulation is real.

Emerging research
◈
Heavy Metals

Lead, mercury, arsenic, and cadmium accumulate in neural tissue over time. Sources range from contaminated water to certain supplement products.

Well-documented
◎
Neurotoxic Pesticides

Organophosphates have demonstrated neurotoxic effects. Dietary and environmental exposure pathways vary widely by region and diet.

Ongoing review
◇
Chronic Stress

Sustained cortisol elevation shrinks hippocampal volume and impairs neuroplasticity. Stress isn't just mental — it's structural brain damage over time.

Strong evidence
○
Sleep Deprivation

The glymphatic system clears amyloid and metabolic waste during deep sleep. Chronic deficit is one of the highest-impact brain threats there is.

Strong evidence
△
Supplement Contamination

Third-party testing reveals heavy metal contamination in a significant share of supplement products. Not all nootropics are what they claim to be.

Consumer risk
