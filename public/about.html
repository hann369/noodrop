The story

How it started.

2022
The beginning

I wanted more.

More focus, more energy, more output. I started reading about nootropics and couldn't stop. Every answer led to five new questions. The rabbit hole was deep — and there was no map.

Every source was scattered, surface-level, or trying to sell me something. I kept notes. A lot of notes.

First stack: Caffeine + L-Theanine 100+ hours of PubMed reading Zero products sold
2023
Self-experimentation

Running protocols.

Systematic self-experimentation. One compound at a time. Logging mood, focus, sleep, and HRV. Learning what the research says vs what actually happens in practice.

Started documenting everything publicly — not as a guru, but as someone figuring it out in real-time.

12+ compounds trialed Daily protocol logs YouTube channel started
2024
Building in public

The platform emerges.

The notes turned into a library. The library needed a stack builder. The stack builder needed interaction checking. One thing led to the next — and Metacognition was born.

Built everything I wished existed when I started. Evidence grades, mechanism explanations, interaction checks — all in one place.

Metacognition library v1 Stack builder launched 50+ compounds documented
2026
Live now

Still building.

Metacognition is live. The marketplace is launching. The community section is coming. And the protocols keep evolving as the research does.

This is the experiment — building a platform while being its most visible user. It doesn't end here.
