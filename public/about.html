The Story

Not a guru.
A work in progress.

It started simply – I wanted more. More focus, more energy, more output. And like most people who fall into this rabbit hole, I found nootropics.

What I didn't find was a single place that combined real information, honest reviews, and actual tools for building your own protocol. Everything was scattered, surface-level, or trying to sell me something.

So I built Noodrop. A library, a stack builder, a marketplace – all in one place. And I document everything publicly under hann369, because I believe the best way to learn is out loud.

This isn't about being perfect. It's about lifemaxx – maximizing your life by going further than most people are willing to go. Nootropics are one tool. I use them, I share what works, and I build the platform around the journey.
