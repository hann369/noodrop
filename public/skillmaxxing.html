The brain responds to deliberate stress — like a muscle.

Every time you switch to a foreign language, execute a new movement, or improvise a melody, you're physically rewiring neural circuitry. Neuroplasticity isn't passive — it's earned.

Compounds can accelerate certain pathways. Sleep and nutrition form the foundation. But the real driver is always intentional, repeated practice.
